Origin of Akan Names

Akan names originate from the Akan people of Ghana and Côte d’Ivoire. These names are traditionally given based on the day of the week a child is born and are believed to reflect a person’s soul, character, and destiny. Male Akan names often begin with “Kw-” while female names begin with “A-”. Each name carries cultural, spiritual, and traditional meaning linked to the day of birth.
